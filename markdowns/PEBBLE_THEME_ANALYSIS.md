# Pebble Theme Analysis: Styles & Patterns

The Pebble Shopify theme is defined by a **"Soft & Organic"** aesthetic. It uses large border radii, muted earthy tones, and a minimalist layout to create a friendly, premium feel.

## 1. Design Philosophy
- **Soft UI / Glassmorphism-lite**: Elements often feel like they are floating on soft-colored canvases.
- **Organic Shapes**: Heavy use of rounded corners (20px to pill-shaped) avoids sharp edges, making the brand feel approachable.
- **Minimalism**: Focus on high-quality imagery with sparse, clean typography.

## 2. Color Palette
| Purpose | Estimated Value | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Background** | `#FFFFFF` | Used for main content areas and header. |
| **Hero Accent** | `#9BA88B` | A muted sage green used for major section backgrounds. |
| **Secondary Accent** | `#F5F5C6` | A soft, pale yellow used for contrast sections. |
| **Tertiary Accent** | `#E1EBF2` | A light powder blue for testimonials or secondary grids. |
| **Text (Primary)** | `#000000` | High-contrast black for headings and body text. |
| **Text (Accent)** | `#666666` | Grey used for secondary labels and counts (e.g., "Sweaters 25"). |
| **Status Badges** | Variable | Muted red (Sale), Blue (Popular), Green (New). |

## 3. Typography
- **Headings (H1, H2)**: 
  - **Family**: Bold Sans-Serif (likely *Inter* or *Outfit*).
  - **Style**: Tight letter-spacing, heavy weight.
  - **Feel**: Modern and impactful.
- **Body & Navigation**:
  - **Family**: Clean Sans-Serif.
  - **Size**: 14px - 16px for UI elements.
  - **Weight**: Regular to Medium.
- **Specialty Fonts**: 
  - **Logo**: A playful, stylized serif/script for "little" and a bold sans for "PEBBLE".

## 4. UI Components & Patterns

### Header & Navigation
- **Floating Design**: The header often appears as a floating white card with large rounded corners.
- **Search Bar**: Integrated "What are you looking for?" text with minimal outline icons.

### Buttons
- **Shape**: Pill-shaped (fully rounded).
- **Style**: 
  - Primary: White background with black text and a circular arrow icon.
  - Secondary: Outlined or text-only with subtle hover states.
- **Shadows**: Subtle, soft drop shadows to create depth.

### Product Cards & Grids
- **Image Radius**: Very high border-radius (approx. `24px` - `32px`).
- **Interaction**: "Add to Cart" appears as a floating pill button at the bottom of the image container.
- **Badges**: Small, rounded pill badges placed in the top-left or top-right corners of images.

### Sections & Layout
- **Split Layouts**: 50/50 splits between lifestyle imagery and bold typography.
- **Testimonial Grid**: Card-based layout on a soft-colored background, using overlapping or staggered arrangements.

## 5. Key Visual Signatures
1. **The "Pebble" Radius**: Almost every container has a radius of at least `20px`.
2. **Soft Shadows**: Elements use a "diffused" shadow rather than a hard one.
3. **Muted Overlays**: Images often have a slight desaturation or soft color overlay to match the theme's palette.
4. **Interactive Arrows**: Navigation arrows (next/prev) are small, thin, and often placed inside circular containers.
